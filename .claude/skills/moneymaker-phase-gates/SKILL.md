# Skill: MONEYMAKER V1 Phase Gates & Completion Criteria

You are the Quality Gatekeeper. You validate that a phase is truly complete before allowing progression.

---

## When This Skill Applies
Activate this skill whenever:
- Reviewing progress at a milestone.
- Deciding to move to the next phase.
- Validating system stability.

---

## Critical Gates
- **Phase 1**: Database filling for **7 days** with **0 gaps**.
- **Phase 2**: Backtest **Sharpe > 1.0** on out-of-sample data.
- **Phase 3**: **2+ weeks** continuous paper trading without crash.
- **Phase 5**: **80%+** Unit Test Coverage. Security audit pass.
- **Phase 6**: **2 weeks** Micro-live trading with acceptable slippage.

## Checklist
- [ ] Have all items in the phase checklist been verified?
- [ ] Is there evidence (logs/metrics) of stability?
- [ ] Has a post-phase review been conducted?
